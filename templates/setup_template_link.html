Quick Tips

Verify Your Link

Double-check that your URL is correct and accessible. Invalid links may cause your automation job to fail.

Privacy Settings

Ensure your post or profile is public. Services can't interact with content that has restricted visibility.

Multiple Links

For best results, create separate automation jobs for different links rather than changing the link mid-campaign.
